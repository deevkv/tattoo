Syndicate Tattoo Studio is a completely new studio format where professionals have the freedom to create and realize themselves.

Advantages

  • Artistic tattoo

    The art tattoo is an imprint of an internal ego on your body. You always choose which picture to capture: be it a philosophical thought, a life-affirming appeal, a hieroglyph or something that specifically symbolizes you.

  • Correction and overlap of tattoos

    Often, in pursuit of a more attractive price or not having complete information about the process and the intricacies of the application, people get a poor quality tattoo. Our masters have extensive practice in solving such problems.

  • Development of individual sketches

    A good tattoo implies an original idea and a unique design. We are against mindless copying and adhere to an individual approach when working with each client.

Comfort and safety

The Tattoo Syndicate Salon is comfortable for customers and employees - there are cozy chairs and couches for visitors where they can relax, as well as comfortable workplaces for craftsmen.

The studio is equipped with the latest technology - it is modern tools and equipment, the best devices for disinfection and sterilization, safe quality paint. It is mandatory to use disposable needles, gloves, caps for paints, etc., after each client, tattoo machines and the workplace of the master are treated with special solutions. Workrooms regularly disinfect with a bactericidal ultraviolet lamp that destroys all microorganisms and bacteria.

All of our artists are licensed by the City of New York Department of Health, and have been educated in the area of blood borne pathogens, cross contamination, and sterilization to ensure a clean and safe environment for everyone.

Prices

Consultation Wizard Sign up for a consultation at a convenient time for you.
Miniature tattoo A miniature tattoo includes images of a format less than 10x10 cm. The procedure for applying such tattoos takes no more than 1 hour and fits into one session. A miniature tattoo can include small characters, patterns and inscriptions with a minimum number of colors and low detail.
Tattoo large size The large size of the tattoo includes work, usually requiring lengthy sessions. The procedure for applying such images takes more than 5 hours. For example, a full-color drawing in the style of "realism", from the shoulder to the bend of the arm, may require a session lasting more than 6 hours. Depending on the complexity and detail of the picture, there may be several such sessions.
Full-length tattoo.The cost of an hour of work. The full-size tattoo includes large-scale work requiring several lengthy sessions. One procedure for applying such images takes more than 30 hours. For example, applying a full-color picture to the entire back, may require 5 to 8 sessions of 6-8 hours each. Typically, such projects are paid at an hourly rate.
